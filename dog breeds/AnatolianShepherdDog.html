Care

Anatolian Shepherd Dogs are hardy working guardians built for outdoor life. Their care focuses on coat maintenance, safe space to patrol, and thoughtful management of their strong guarding instincts.

General Care

  • Brush the coat weekly; during shedding season, daily brushing helps manage loose hair.
  • Check ears regularly for debris or redness, especially if they spend time outdoors.
  • Trim nails every few weeks; large dogs can struggle if nails become too long.
  • Provide sturdy, comfortable bedding and shade if they rest outside.

Fun Facts

  • They originated on the harsh plateaus of Anatolia, guarding flocks from wolves and other predators.
  • Many Anatolians will calmly place themselves between danger and the animals or people they protect.
  • They are naturally alert at night, patrolling and watching over their territory.
  • In some conservation projects, Anatolian Shepherds are used to protect livestock from cheetahs and reduce conflict.
  • Though serious on duty, they can be gentle, loving giants with their own families.

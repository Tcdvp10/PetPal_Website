Spinning Around and Around and Around Before Lying Down

You have seen them do it and maybe muttered a ‚ÄúJust get on with it and lay down‚Äù during the act. It turns out dogs spin around and around ‚Ä¶ and around some more ‚Ä¶ for a reason. In the wild, dogs did not have a cozy bed or loving human to cuddle up with while they went to sleep. In order to give themselves a comfortable place to rest, they would spin around to mat down grass and to kick up anything that might get in their way, like bugs, dirt, sticks, or anything else you would find in nature. Today, spinning around helps them to create a comfortable nest, whether it is in a dog bed or a blanket pile.

Licking Face

Licking Face

Anyone who has ever been around a dog has been subject to dog kisses. Whether you love them or not, you are going to be on the receiving end of some slobber. Dogs kiss you for many reasons. For one, it is a way they would communicate in the wild. It is also the way a mother communicates with her young. She licks them to clean them or stimulate them to breathe when they are born. Another reason is one many people already think: the dogs are showing affection. Licking their guardian releases endorphins that can comfort dogs. The next time your dog gives you big kisses, they just want you to know they really like being around you.

Wagging their Tails

Wagging their Tails

Like licking, tail wagging is another sign of communication between dogs. Not all tail wagging is happy, however. Dogs can also wag their tails when they are frightened. You can tell, however, how a dog is feeling by looking at the way their tail wags. If you see it wagging to the right, that means they are happy. When they are frightened, they wag their tails to the left.

  • Puppies: 3‚Äì4 small meals per day with puppy-formula food.

Feeding & Nutrition

A balanced diet keeps your dog energized, supports healthy digestion, and helps maintain a shiny coat and strong immune system.

  • Puppies: 3‚Äì4 small meals per day with puppy-formula food.
  • Adults: 2 meals per day, ideally at the same times each day.
  • Seniors: lower calories with extra joint and digestive support.
  • Measure meals with a cup instead of guessing to prevent obesity.
  • Introduce new food over 5‚Äì7 days to avoid stomach upset.
  • Always provide fresh, clean water in a washed bowl.
  • Avoid dangerous foods like chocolate, grapes, raisins, onions, and xylitol.

Comfort & Sleep

Dogs need a calm, cozy space to relax. Good sleep helps them recover, learn, and stay in a good mood.

  • Choose a soft, supportive bed sized for your dog.
  • Place the bed away from drafts, loud doors, and busy walkways.
  • Keep a simple bedtime routine: potty break ‚Üí water ‚Üí lights down.
  • Puppies may sleep 18‚Äì20 hours a day ‚Äî this is normal while they grow.
